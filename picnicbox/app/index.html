picnic box group

The picnic box, our mobile catering trailer, offers British food for festivals, public or private events and corporate functions.

We serve a range of toasted sandwiches, homemade cakes, homemade quiches, sausage rolls, pork pies and scotch eggs. We serve up to 20 different flavours of milkshakes, smoothies, loose leaf teas and coffee. We also have a large selection of gluten free products!

For more information or to book us for an event please contact us thepicnicboxgroup@gmail.com or take a look at some of our recent events on facebook.com/thepicnicboxgroup
